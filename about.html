SPINNING OUT CREPES AND PULLING GREAT COFFEE SINCE 1999



We serve rich coffee and have full menu with freshly prepared crepes, sandwiches, omelettes, and pastries. When we can, we use organic, Fairtrade, Irish produce that's fresh and every dish we make is cooked from scratch right in front of you.
It's not just lip service; we’ve stuck to our goal over the years. We still cook from scratch, sourcing free range, fair trade, organic and the highest quality ingredients in the process. All this to make sure you have the best quality food, as fresh as we can make it. We believe eating out is about more than just great food and that a warm and welcoming atmosphere makes each bite taste that little bit better. Our menu has great choices to suit every taste from dawn to dusk. So whether you're looking for a full refuel or a comfort moment, we have anytime food for everyone. Drop in, we’ll be delighted to see you.
